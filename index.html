Nice and friendly Office Assistant

OABot isn't intended to replace an office assistant, but it can do the hard work and bring a whole new working environment to a small and medium office. OABot can welcome guests and client, guide them to the corresponding room. Having a new employee, OABot can introduce her with an office tour. OABot can also deliver mails/packages to a target room or staff, operate copy-fax machine, and a lot of office works. Built with face-recognition, speech-synthesis system, OABot becomes a friendly office assistant. Moreover, OABot brings a whole new office environment and collaboration experience to every staff.

Technical Overview

Route-Tracking allows OABot to easily travel through an office by learning a floor plan. With Collision Detection, she can avoid hitting staff or furniture.

OABot has a Robotic arm which is strong for heavy objects, and precise for small items. Built-in camera with face-recognition, OABot can distinguish all staff members, communicates with a synthesized speech. She can also learn new phases and sentences. Speed made by OABot is not simply a recorded voice but more interactive, and friendly conversation.
